Simple Breakfast Burrito

About this recipe

This is just an extremely simple breakfast burrito I've made many times. It is quick, easy, and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 large burrito-style tortilla
  • 3 eggs, whisked
  • 3 slices of bacon
  • 1/2 diced bell pepper
  • 1/2 diced tomato
  • Your choice of cheese
  • Hatch 505 Green Chile, to taste

Steps

  1. Lightly grease and heat medium pan, add eggs, tomato, and bell pepper, stir often until until scrambled
  2. Add cheese to eggs and mix, emplace lid to ensure cheese melts
  3. Cook bacon according to instructions on package
  4. Add bacon to eggs and mix
  5. Place egg mixture in tortilla, add green chile, and roll the burrito
  6. ENJOY!
