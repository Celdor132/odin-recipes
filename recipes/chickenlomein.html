Chicken Lo Mein

About this recipe

This is a deicious Chicken Lo Mein recipe that my wife makes for me. It is quick, simple, and much healthier than getting takeout! It was created by her to help me eat healthy, while still being able to indulge in my favorite meal. 4 servings

Ingredients

Noodles and Vegetables

  • 1/3 package "Simply Asia Chinese Style Lo Mein"
  • 1/2 lb diced raw boneless chicken (thighs preferrred)
  • 1 tbsp oil (vegetable, canola, or peanut)
  • 1/2 cup snowpeas
  • 1/2 cup green beans
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1 sliced bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped mushrooms

Sauce

  • 1/2 cup reduced sodium soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup hoisin sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 3 tsp Sriracha

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine soy sauce, hoisin, sesame oil, and Sriracha
  2. Bring large pot of water to boil, and cook noodles according to package
  3. Add chicken to large greased pan, and cook unti complete- 7-10 minutes- season to taste.
  4. Cook vegetables
    1. In a large pan, heat sesame oil
    2. Add carrots, bell pepper, green beans and mushrooms, cook for 3-4 minutes
    3. Add snowpeas, cook until tender
    4. Remove from heat, leave lid on to maintain temperature
  5. Add chicken, vegetables, and sauce to the noodle pot, gently stir
  6. Serve and enjoy! Refrigerate leftovers
